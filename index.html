EAC TrustChain

A blockchain-powered platform improving cross-border payments, farmer financial inclusion, medicine verification, and digital education across East Africa.

View Prototype Learn More

The Problem

Many communities in East Africa face financial exclusion, high transfer costs, lack of transparency, and safety risks in healthcare and digital finance.

High Cross-Border Fees

High transaction costs in East Africa limit financial access.

Farmers Lack Records

Farmers struggle without digital financial history.

Counterfeit Medicines

Fake drugs harm communities and trust in healthcare.

Limited Digital Education

Users have low awareness of safe digital finance.

Our Solution

EAC TrustChain uses blockchain technology (simulation prototype) to create transparent financial records, enable safer cross-border transactions, verify medical supply authenticity, and educate users on responsible digital finance.

Cross-Border Payments

Low-cost, fast simulated transfers across East Africa.

Farmer Financial Ledger

Blockchain-based transaction history to build financial trust.

Medicine Verification

Verify medical batch authenticity through QR scanning (simulation).

Education & Rewards

Interactive lessons on crypto safety with demo token rewards.
